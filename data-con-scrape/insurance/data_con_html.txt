      <h3 style="text-align: center;"><strong>OVERVIEW OF SESSIONS</strong></h3>
<h4>The schedule is primarily organized by programming languages and themes.  Traditionally, we&#8217;ve had separate R, Python, or Hadoop events; however this year we are having them all at one event.  Some topics, such as Data Engineering or Data Visualization, span several languages, and as such we believe it will be beneficial for folks of different programming language background to see what is possible on different platforms.   Albeit, all that said, R is more on Saturday, and Python on Sunday as we realize folks often are interested in one or the other.</h4>
